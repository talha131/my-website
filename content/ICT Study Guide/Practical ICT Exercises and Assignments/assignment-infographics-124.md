## Objective

Create a checklist infographic in Canva that lists the warning signs of online scams and tells readers exactly what to do when they spot each one.

## Instructions

### Red Flags to Include

Present each warning sign as a checklist item with a red flag icon and a one-line action step:

1. **The message is from an unknown number or address.** Action: Do not reply until you have verified who it is.
2. **It asks for your OTP, password, or bank details.** Action: Stop immediately. No legitimate organization asks for these by message or call.
3. **It promises an unexpected prize, gift, or large sum of money.** Action: Ignore and delete. If it sounds too good to be true, it is.
4. **It creates urgency: "Act now," "Your account will be blocked," "Only 10 minutes left."** Action: Slow down. Scammers want you to panic. Take your time and verify first.
5. **The link address has spelling errors or an unusual domain.** Action: Do not click. Go to the organization's official website directly by typing the address yourself.
6. **A caller claims to be from your bank and asks you to confirm your card number.** Action: Hang up. Call your bank directly using the number on the back of your card.
7. **A WhatsApp message forwards a "shocking news" story or a free offer.** Action: Verify through a reliable news source before believing or sharing it.
8. **A job offer asks for a registration or processing fee before hiring.** Action: Legitimate employers do not charge you to apply. This is a scam.

### What to Do If You Think You Have Been Scammed

Add a separate box with steps:
1. Do not send any more money or information.
2. Change your passwords immediately.
3. Tell a trusted adult or family member.
4. Report to FIA Cybercrime Wing (Pakistan): cia@fia.gov.pk or call 1991.

### Design in Canva

- Checklist format with a red flag or warning icon for each item.
- Two columns: "Warning Sign" and "What to Do."
- Use red and white as the main colors.
- The "What to Do If Scammed" box at the bottom in a contrasting color.

### Required Elements

- At least 7 warning signs with action steps.
- A "What to Do If Scammed" box with the FIA Cybercrime contact.
- Title: "Warning Signs of Online Scams."
