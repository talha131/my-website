Healthy Snack Ideas

## Objective

Create an infographic about healthy snack ideas.

## Instructions

- Choose a clean background that fits the theme of healthy eating. Consider using images of fresh fruits, vegetables, or a simple geometric pattern.
- Title: Clearly state the purpose of the infographic, such as "Healthy Snack Ideas."
- Categories: Organize snacks into categories like fruits, vegetables, nuts and seeds, dairy, and whole grains.
- Images: Include clear images of each snack to make the infographic appealing and easy to browse.
- Benefits: Cover the nutritional benefits of each snack, such as vitamins, minerals, and fiber content.
- Serving Size: Provide recommended serving sizes to support portion control.
- Preparation Tips: Include simple instructions on how to prepare each snack, if needed.
- Nutritional Information: Display key nutritional information, such as calories, protein, carbohydrates, and fats, for each snack.
- Layout: Use a clean and organized layout with easy-to-read fonts and colors.
- Visual Hierarchy: Use different font sizes, colors, or icons to draw attention to important information.
- White Space: Leave enough white space to prevent overcrowding and support readability.
- Consistency: Keep a consistent design style throughout the infographic.
