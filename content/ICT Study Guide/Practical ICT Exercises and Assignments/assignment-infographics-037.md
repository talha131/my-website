Boosting self-esteem and overcoming negativity.

## Objective

Create an infographic about boosting self-esteem and overcoming negativity.

## Instructions

- Identify the key topics: what self-esteem is, common sources of negativity, and methods for building confidence.
- Research reliable sources, including psychology journals, self-help books, and reputable websites.
- Use Canva to design the infographic.
- Plan the layout, color scheme, and typography before you begin.

## Background

- Explain what self-esteem is and why it matters for mental well-being.
- Note common sources of negativity, such as self-doubt and comparison to others.
- Briefly mention how low self-esteem can affect relationships, work, and daily life.

## Key Elements

- **Positive Affirmations:** Examples of statements people can use to challenge negative thoughts.
- **Self-Care Practices:** Activities that support emotional well-being, such as exercise, mindfulness, and hobbies.
- **Cognitive-Behavioral Techniques:** Simple strategies for identifying and changing unhelpful thought patterns.
- **Building Resilience:** Tips for handling setbacks, such as focusing on personal strengths and learning from mistakes.

## Presentation

- Design a layout that matches the theme of confidence and positivity.
- Use bright colors and clear graphics to support readability.
- Keep text short and direct - bullet points or short sentences work well.
- Include your sources to support the information provided.
