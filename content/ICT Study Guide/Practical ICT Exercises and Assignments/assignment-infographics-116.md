## Objective

Create a categorized infographic in Canva that covers the four main types of pollution, with causes, health and environmental effects, and examples relevant to Pakistan.

## Instructions

### Four Sections - One Per Pollution Type

**Air Pollution**
- Causes: vehicle exhaust, factory emissions, open burning of garbage, generator fumes, construction dust
- Effects: respiratory disease (asthma, bronchitis), smog, reduced visibility, climate change
- Local example: Karachi's air quality is frequently among the worst in major Asian cities due to traffic, industry, and generator use

**Water Pollution**
- Causes: industrial waste dumped into rivers, untreated sewage, plastic waste, agricultural runoff (pesticides and fertilizers)
- Effects: contaminated drinking water, skin and stomach diseases, death of aquatic life
- Local example: Lyari River and Hub River receive untreated industrial and sewage discharge

**Soil Pollution**
- Causes: plastic and solid waste dumping, overuse of chemical fertilizers and pesticides, industrial waste disposal
- Effects: reduced soil fertility, contamination of crops, groundwater pollution
- Local example: open dumping sites in many areas of Karachi, particularly near low-income neighborhoods

**Noise Pollution**
- Causes: heavy traffic, construction, loudspeakers, industrial machinery, wedding events in residential areas
- Effects: hearing loss, sleep disruption, stress and anxiety, reduced concentration in students
- Local example: traffic congestion on main roads during rush hours; generator noise in load-shedding areas

### Design in Canva

- Four equal panels or quadrants, one per pollution type.
- Each panel: a title, a relevant icon, causes listed as brief bullet points, effects listed, and one local example.
- Use distinct colors for each type: grey for air, blue for water, brown for soil, orange for noise.

### Required Elements

- All four pollution types covered.
- Causes, effects, and a local Pakistan/Karachi example for each.
- Color-coded panels with icons.
- Title: "Types of Pollution."
