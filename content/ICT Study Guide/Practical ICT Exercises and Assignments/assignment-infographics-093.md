## Objective

Create a timeline infographic in Canva showing every PSL champion from 2016 to the most recent season.

## Important Note on Accuracy

Look up PSL results online before adding any data. Verify the winning team, runner-up, final city, and winning captain for each season. The PSL has six teams: Karachi Kings, Lahore Qalandars, Islamabad United, Peshawar Zalmi, Quetta Gladiators, and Multan Sultans.

## Instructions

### Step 1: Research

For each PSL season from 2016 onwards, find:

- Season number and year
- Winning team
- Runner-up
- Final city and venue
- Winning captain

### Step 2: Design the Timeline

Arrange seasons in order from 2016 to the most recent. For each entry:

- Season number and year
- Winning team (using the team's colors where possible)
- Runner-up
- Final venue
- Winning captain's name

At the end, add a count showing how many titles each team has won.

### Step 3: Build in Canva

- Use a vertical or horizontal timeline layout.
- Match each entry card to the winning team's colors.
- Add cricket icons: a bat, a ball, a trophy.

### Required Elements

- All PSL seasons up to the most recent included.
- Winner, runner-up, venue, and captain for each season.
- Total title count per team shown in the design.
- Data verified online before submission.
