The impact of social media on mental health.

## Objective

Create an infographic about the impact of social media on mental health.

## Instructions

- Define the purpose and audience before you start.
- Find data and statistics on social media and mental health from reputable sources.
- Decide which key points and findings to include.

## Background

- Give a brief overview of how widely social media is used across different age groups.
- Note some benefits of social media, such as staying connected and sharing information.

## Key Elements

- **Statistics and Data:** Use numbers to show the link between social media use and mental health outcomes such as anxiety, depression, or cyberbullying.
- **Visuals:** Use charts, graphs, and icons to present the data clearly.
- **Tips for Healthy Use:** Give specific, actionable steps such as setting screen time limits, taking breaks, and knowing when to seek support.

## Presentation

- Design a clear layout with a logical order of information. Choose colors and typography that are easy to read.
- Add relevant images or illustrations to support the text.
- Keep the infographic focused - do not try to cover everything.
- Use accessible fonts and sufficient color contrast.
