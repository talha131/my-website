## Objective

Create a timeline infographic in Canva showing every Olympic gold medal Pakistan has won, from the first in 1960 through to the most recent Olympics.

## Important Note on Accuracy

Look up Pakistan's full Olympic history online before starting. Verify the year, sport, host city, and athlete or team details for each gold medal. Note: Pakistan's Olympic gold medals have been in field hockey - confirm this through your research and update if anything has changed.

## Instructions

### Step 1: Research

Find:

- Every year Pakistan won an Olympic gold medal
- The sport
- Host city and year of the Olympics
- Names of key players or the team captain
- Final score if available

Also record which Olympics Pakistan attended but did not win gold - include these as context markers on the timeline.

### Step 2: Design the Timeline

For each gold medal entry:

- Olympic year and host city
- Sport
- Captain or key players
- Score in the final

Mark gold medal years with a gold star or trophy icon. Mark other years of participation with a smaller, neutral marker.

### Step 3: Build in Canva

- Use a vertical or horizontal timeline layout.
- Use Pakistan's green and white as the main colors.
- Add a hockey stick and ball icon to each gold entry.

### Required Elements

- All Olympic gold medals with year, sport, host city, and key players.
- Full participation history shown for context, with gold years clearly distinguished.
- Title: "Pakistan's Olympic Gold Medals."
