## Objective

Create a timeline infographic in Canva showing every ICC Men's T20 World Cup tournament from 2007 to 2024.

## Important Note on Accuracy

Look up the T20 World Cup results online before adding any data. Verify the winning team, runner-up, host country, and year for each tournament. Do not rely on memory.

## Instructions

### Step 1: Research

Find the following for each T20 World Cup (2007, 2009, 2010, 2012, 2014, 2016, 2021, 2022, 2024):

- Host country
- Winning team
- Runner-up
- Final venue

Note Pakistan's result in each tournament.

### Step 2: Design the Timeline

Arrange tournaments chronologically from 2007 to 2024. For each entry include:

- Year
- Host country (with flag)
- Winner (with flag)
- Runner-up

Mark Pakistan's 2009 win with a special highlight, badge, or star.

### Step 3: Build in Canva

- Use a horizontal or vertical timeline layout.
- Add cricket-themed icons: stumps, bat, ball.
- Alternate card colors slightly to make each year easy to distinguish.

### Design Tips

- Keep each tournament entry concise: year, host, winner, runner-up.
- Use flag icons to make countries easy to identify quickly.

### Required Elements

- All T20 World Cup editions from 2007 to 2024.
- Winner and runner-up for each edition.
- Pakistan's 2009 title clearly highlighted.
- Data verified online before submission.
