## Objective

Create a timeline infographic in Canva showing important inventions from the 1600s to 2020, in chronological order.

## Important Note on Accuracy

Look up each invention online before adding it. Verify the inventor's name, nationality, and year. Some inventions have disputed origins or multiple contributors - note this where relevant.

## Instructions

### Step 1: Choose Your Inventions

Select 15 to 20 inventions spread across the full period. Cover a range of fields: science, communication, transport, medicine, and computing. Some inventions to research:

- Telescope (1608), Calculus (Newton/Leibniz, 1660s-1680s), Steam engine (Watt, 1769), Vaccination (Jenner, 1796), Photography (Daguerre/Niepce, 1826), Telegraph (Morse, 1837), Telephone (Bell, 1876), Light bulb (Edison/Swan, 1879), Radio (Marconi, 1895), Airplane (Wright Brothers, 1903), Penicillin (Fleming, 1928), Television (Baird/others, 1920s), Electronic computer (1940s), DNA structure (Watson, Crick, Franklin, 1953), Internet (ARPANET, 1969), World Wide Web (Berners-Lee, 1991), Smartphone (2007)

### Step 2: For Each Invention Include

- Year
- Name of invention
- Inventor's name and nationality
- One line on what it changed

### Step 3: Build in Canva

- Horizontal timeline from 1600 to 2020.
- Mark century boundaries (1700, 1800, 1900, 2000) for visual structure.
- Use a small icon or illustration for each invention.
- Alternate entries above and below the timeline axis to avoid crowding.

### Required Elements

- At least 15 inventions spread across the full time range.
- Year, inventor, and one-line description for each.
- Century divisions marked clearly on the axis.
- Title: "Important Inventions: 1600-2020."
