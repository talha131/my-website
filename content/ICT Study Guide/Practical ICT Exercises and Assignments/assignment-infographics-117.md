## Objective

Create a fishbone diagram in Canva that maps the root causes of air pollution in Karachi, organized by category.

## Instructions

### Step 1: Define the Problem

Write this at the fish head (right side):

**"Air pollution in Karachi is worsening"**

### Step 2: Identify Cause Categories

Use these as your main bones:

- **Transport:** Old vehicles without emission controls, two-stroke rickshaws and motorbikes, diesel trucks and buses, traffic congestion causing engines to idle for long periods
- **Industry:** Factories releasing smoke and chemicals, brick kilns on the city outskirts, power plants burning furnace oil
- **Waste Burning:** Open burning of household garbage, burning of plastic and rubber waste in informal settlements
- **Generators:** Widespread use of diesel and petrol generators due to load-shedding, concentrated in commercial and residential areas
- **Construction:** Dust from road construction, building demolition, and large development projects with no dust control measures
- **Urban Vegetation Loss:** Cutting of trees reduces the capacity to absorb pollutants; fewer green spaces in densely built areas

### Step 3: Add Sub-causes

For each category, add two or three specific sub-causes. For example, under Transport: "most rickshaws have no catalytic converter" or "no mandatory annual vehicle emission testing."

### Step 4: Build in Canva

- Fishbone structure with the problem on the right.
- Main categories branch off the central spine.
- Sub-causes on shorter branches.
- One color per main category.

### Design Tips

- Use a smoggy grey-brown color palette to match the topic.
- Bold the main category names.
- Keep each label to a short phrase.

### Required Elements

- Problem statement at the fish head.
- At least 5 main cause categories.
- At least 2 sub-causes per category.
- Title: "Causes of Air Pollution in Karachi."
