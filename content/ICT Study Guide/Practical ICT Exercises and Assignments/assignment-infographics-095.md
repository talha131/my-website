## Objective

Create a timeline infographic in Canva that maps major art movements from 1400 to 2000, showing when each began and ended, what defined it, and who its key artists were.

## Important Note on Accuracy

Look up each movement online before adding dates or descriptions. Art movement dates overlap and are approximate - note this where relevant in your design.

## Instructions

### Step 1: Research the Movements

Find approximate dates, one defining characteristic, and one or two key artists for each of these movements:

- Renaissance (approx. 1400-1600)
- Baroque (approx. 1600-1750)
- Neoclassicism (approx. 1750-1850)
- Romanticism (approx. 1780-1850)
- Impressionism (approx. 1860-1890)
- Post-Impressionism (approx. 1880-1910)
- Expressionism (approx. 1905-1930s)
- Cubism (approx. 1907-1920s)
- Surrealism (approx. 1920s-1940s)
- Abstract Expressionism (approx. 1940s-1950s)
- Pop Art (approx. 1950s-1960s)
- Postmodernism (approx. 1970s-1990s)

### Step 2: For Each Movement Include

- Name and approximate date range
- One defining characteristic (e.g., "Used light and everyday scenes" for Impressionism)
- Key artist(s) and one famous work

### Step 3: Build in Canva

- Horizontal timeline from 1400 to 2000.
- Use a distinct background color per movement era.
- Show overlapping date ranges where movements coexisted.
- Add a small image or illustration representative of each movement's style.

### Required Elements

- At least 10 art movements with dates, characteristics, and key artists.
- A small visual for each movement.
- Overlapping date ranges shown where applicable.
- Title: "Major Art Movements: 1400-2000."
