## Objective

Create a concept map in Canva that organizes the main teaching methods and shows how they relate to each other.

## Instructions

### Central Concept

Place **"Teaching Methods"** at the center of your map.

### Main Branches

Connect at least five teaching methods to the central concept:

- Lecture Method
- Discussion Method
- Demonstration Method
- Cooperative Learning
- Inquiry-Based Learning
- Project-Based Learning

### For Each Method, Add Nodes For:

- A short definition (one phrase)
- When it works best
- One advantage
- One limitation

### Design in Canva

- Use Canva's shapes (circles, rectangles) and connector lines to build the map.
- The central node should be larger than the surrounding nodes.
- Use one color per method to group its related nodes.
- Add labeled arrows between related methods where they overlap or connect.
- Keep text inside each node to one to five words.

### Required Elements

- At least 5 teaching methods connected to the center.
- At least 3 nodes per method (definition, best use, and one advantage or limitation).
- Color-coded branches by method.
- Title: "Teaching Methods."
