## Objective

Create an infographic in Canva that covers the plot, important events, and main characters of "Home Alone" (1990).

## Instructions

Watch "Home Alone" (1990) if you haven't seen it recently. Take notes on the main plot points, key events, and important characters.

### Plan Your Infographic

- Outline the main characters: Kevin, the burglars, and Kevin's family.
- Identify 5-7 major plot points, including Kevin's tricks and traps.
- Note key locations like Kevin's house and the surrounding neighborhood.

### Design in Canva

- Use holiday-themed icons (Christmas tree, presents) and household items Kevin uses as traps.
- Use festive colors - red, green, and gold - to reflect the Christmas setting.
- Choose a playful font for headings and a clear font for details.
- Use a timeline to show the sequence of events from being left behind to reuniting with family.

### Content to Include

- Movie title "Home Alone" and release year (1990).
- Brief descriptions of the main characters, especially Kevin and the burglars.
- A memorable quote such as "Keep the change, ya filthy animal."
- The central themes of family and resourcefulness.

### Final Review

- Check for spelling and grammar mistakes.
- Confirm that all information accurately reflects the movie's plot.
- Make sure the infographic is easy to read.
