Stars lifecycle.

## Objective

Create an infographic about the lifecycle of stars.

## Instructions

- Research the stages of a star's lifecycle: formation, main sequence, red giant, supernova, and what follows depending on the star's mass.
- Choose a format for your infographic.
- Use Canva to build it.
- Outline the key points for each stage before you begin designing.
- Arrange the information so it flows clearly from one stage to the next.

## Background

- Briefly introduce the idea of stellar evolution.
- Explain the role of gravity and nuclear fusion in a star's lifecycle.
- Include a few key facts about stars, such as their size, temperature, or lifespan.

## Key Elements

- Diagrams or illustrations of each stage of the lifecycle.
- Short explanations of the key processes at each stage.
- Labels and annotations to help the viewer understand the diagram.
- Comparative data showing how a star's mass or temperature changes through its lifecycle.

## Presentation

- Use a clear layout with colors and fonts that work well together.
- Keep the design uncluttered so the information is easy to read.
- Use a logical sequence with clear transitions between stages.
- Add headings and arrows to guide the viewer through the infographic.
