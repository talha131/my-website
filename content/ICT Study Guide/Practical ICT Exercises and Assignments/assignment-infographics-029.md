Recycling guide.

## Objective

Create an infographic about recycling.

## Background

- Use earthy tones or green hues to match the environmental theme. Consider adding recycling symbols or nature imagery as background elements.

## Key Elements

- **Title:** Make it clear and direct, such as "Recycling Guide" or "Reduce, Reuse, Recycle."
- **Sections:** Divide the infographic by material type: paper, plastic, glass, and metal.
- **Icons:** Use recognizable icons for each recyclable material.
- **Statistics:** Add facts about recycling rates or environmental impact.
- **Tips:** Give practical advice for recycling effectively, such as how to clean and sort materials.
- **Process:** Explain how recycling works, from collection through reprocessing.
- **Call to Action:** Encourage readers to start recycling or reduce their waste.

## Presentation

- Use font size, color, and style to create a clear visual order that guides the reader's eye.
- Leave enough white space to keep the layout readable.
- Add arrows or symbols to show processes or connect related information.
- Keep fonts, colors, and icons consistent across the whole infographic.

## Instructions

- Write instructions in plain language that anyone can understand.
- If showing a process, use numbered steps or a clear sequence.
- Add diagrams or illustrations where they help clarify a step.
- Make each instruction specific enough that the reader knows exactly what to do.
