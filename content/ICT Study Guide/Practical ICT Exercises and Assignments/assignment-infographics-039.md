Common exercise types and their benefits.

## Objective

Create an infographic about common exercise types and their benefits.

## Instructions

1. **Research:** Look into common exercise types and their benefits, including aerobic, strength training, flexibility, and balance exercises.
2. **Gather Data:** Collect data from fitness journals, health organizations, and exercise science studies.
3. **Select a Tool:** Use Canva to design the infographic.
4. **Layout and Design:** Plan the layout, color scheme, and typography for clarity.

## Background

1. **Define Exercise:** Explain how regular physical activity supports both physical and mental health.
2. **Overview:** Describe the four main exercise types: aerobic (cardio), strength training, flexibility (stretching), and balance.
3. **Importance:** Note why including different types of exercise in a routine benefits overall health.

## Key Elements

1. **Exercise Types:** Name and describe each type with examples of activities.
2. **Benefits:** For each type, state the specific benefit - for example, improved cardiovascular health for aerobic exercise, or better range of motion for flexibility training.
3. **Recommendations:** Include general guidance on how often and how long to do each type of exercise.

## Tips for Success

Give practical advice for staying consistent: setting realistic goals, staying hydrated, and paying attention to how your body feels.

## Presentation

1. **Visual Appeal:** Use a clear layout with logical sections.
2. **Visuals:** Add icons or illustrations to explain each exercise type.
3. **Clarity:** Use short text, bullet points, or numbered lists.
4. **Statistics:** Include a relevant data point or two to support the benefits described.
