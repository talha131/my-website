## Objective

Create an informative infographic that presents the story of Prophet Yusuf (Joseph) عليه السلام. Map out the key events of his life.

## Instructions

1. **Research:** Familiarize yourself with his life. Pay attention to its major events, historical context, and key messages.
2. **Planning Your Infographics:** Outline the main elements you want to include in your infographic.
3. Designing Your Infographics:
    1. **Select a Design Tool:** Use [Canva](https://www.canva.com/) for your design.
    2. **Visual Elements:** Select imagery and icons that are symbolic of the key elements and themes in the story. Avoid direct representations of the Prophets to follow Islamic principles.
    3. **Color and Typography:** Choose colors that reflect the mood of the story or its themes. Use typography that supports readability and complements the overall design.
    4. **Layout:** Organize your information logically and attractively.
4. **Inclusion of Verses:** Incorporate relevant verses in Arabic and with translations. Including these verses can strengthen the narrative and provide authentic sources for the depicted events.
5. **Cultural and Religious Sensitivity:** Be mindful of the cultural and religious significance of the story. Ensure your infographic is respectful and presents the material in a way that is appropriate and informative.
