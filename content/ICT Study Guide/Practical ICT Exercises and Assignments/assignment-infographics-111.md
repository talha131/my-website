## Objective

Create a timeline infographic in Canva presenting key events in the life of Prophet Ibrahim (عليه السلام), with Quranic references for each event.

## Instructions

### Events to Include

Place these events in chronological order on the timeline:

1. **Born in Babylon (present-day Iraq)** - Grew up in a society that worshipped idols.
2. **Questioned idol worship** - As a young man, he recognized that idols made of stone could not be gods. Reference: Surah Al-An'am (6:74-79).
3. **Smashed the idols in the temple** - He broke all the idols except the largest one, to make his people think. Reference: Surah Al-Anbiya (21:57-67).
4. **Thrown into the fire by Nimrod** - King Nimrod ordered him to be burned. Allah commanded the fire: "Be cool and safe for Ibrahim." Reference: Surah Al-Anbiya (21:68-69).
5. **Migration from Babylon** - Left his homeland and people after being rejected.
6. **Left his wife Hajar and son Ismail in the valley of Makkah** - Under Allah's command. This act led to the well of Zamzam appearing. Reference: Surah Ibrahim (14:37).
7. **The dream: commanded to sacrifice his son Ismail** - Ibrahim saw in a dream that he was slaughtering his son. He told Ismail, who submitted willingly. Reference: Surah As-Saffat (37:102).
8. **The test passed - a ram was sent** - At the moment of sacrifice, Allah called out that Ibrahim had fulfilled the dream and sent a ram in place of Ismail. Reference: Surah As-Saffat (37:103-107). This event is commemorated on Eid ul-Adha.
9. **Building of the Kaaba** - Ibrahim and Ismail built the Kaaba in Makkah as a house of worship for Allah. Reference: Surah Al-Baqarah (2:127).
10. **Given the title Khalilullah** - Allah named him His close friend. Reference: Surah An-Nisa (4:125).

### Design in Canva

- Horizontal or vertical timeline.
- Each event gets a numbered card with the event title, a brief description, and the Quranic reference.
- Use symbolic imagery: flames for the fire, a crescent for the dream, the Kaaba outline for the final event.
- Keep descriptions brief - two or three sentences per event.

### Required Elements

- All 10 events in correct sequence.
- Quranic reference for each event.
- Symbolic icons used throughout.
- Title: "The Life of Prophet Ibrahim (عليه السلام)."
