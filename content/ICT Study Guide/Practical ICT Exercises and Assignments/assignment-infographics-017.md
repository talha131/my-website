The Evolution of Smartphones: A Journey from 2000 to 2024

## Objective

Create an infographic that outlines the technological advancements and major milestones in the evolution of smartphones from the year 2000 to 2024. Cover key innovations, the introduction of new features, and how these developments changed user experience and everyday life.

## Instructions

### Background Research

- Begin with an exploration of the history of smartphones, starting from the early 2000s. Identify landmark models that introduced new technologies or features.
- Investigate how operating systems (like iOS and Android) have evolved over time.
- Key Elements to include
  - **Timeline:** Design a chronological timeline that marks significant releases and technological breakthroughs in the smartphone industry.
  - **Innovations:** Focus on innovations such as touchscreens, camera technology, app ecosystems, connectivity improvements (3G to 5G), and the integration of artificial intelligence.
  - **Cultural Impact:** Briefly discuss how smartphones changed social interactions, business, and access to information.

### Designing Your Infographics

1. Use [Canva](https://www.canva.com/) to design your infographic. Choose from its templates to accommodate a timeline and various data visualizations.
2. Use icons and images representative of different smartphone models, operating systems, and key features. A color scheme that reflects technology works well here - blues, grays, and greens are common choices.
3. Make the timeline the central element of your infographic, with clear markers for each year or period. Use separate sections to go deeper into specific innovations or impacts.
4. Design the infographic so that someone with no technical background in smartphone technology can follow it easily.
5. Cite your sources at the bottom of the infographic.
