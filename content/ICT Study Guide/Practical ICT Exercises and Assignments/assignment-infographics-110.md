## Objective

Create a step-by-step infographic in Canva showing how to perform wudu (ablution) correctly, in the order prescribed in Islamic practice.

## Instructions

### The Steps of Wudu

Design one numbered panel for each step:

1. **Niyyah (Intention)** - Make the intention in your heart to purify yourself for prayer. The niyyah is not spoken aloud in Sunni practice.
2. **Bismillah** - Begin by saying "Bismillah" (In the name of Allah).
3. **Wash both hands** - Wash up to and including the wrists three times, starting with the right hand.
4. **Rinse the mouth** - Take water into the mouth and rinse three times.
5. **Clean the nose** - Sniff water into the nostrils and blow it out three times.
6. **Wash the face** - Wash the entire face (from forehead to chin, ear to ear) three times.
7. **Wash the arms** - Wash the right arm from fingertips to elbow three times, then the left arm three times.
8. **Masah (wipe the head)** - Wipe the head once with wet hands, passing from front to back.
9. **Wipe the ears** - Using the same water, wipe the inside and outside of both ears once.
10. **Wash the feet** - Wash the right foot up to and including the ankle three times, starting between the toes, then the left foot three times.

### Add a Short Note

Include a small note box listing what breaks wudu:
- Using the toilet
- Passing wind
- Deep sleep
- Loss of consciousness
- Bleeding heavily

### Design in Canva

- Ten numbered panels arranged in a grid or vertical sequence.
- Each panel: a step number, the action name, a one-sentence instruction, and a simple body diagram or icon showing which part of the body is being cleaned.
- Use blue and white as the main colors (water theme).
- Right-hand actions should be clearly distinguished as first.

### Required Elements

- All 10 steps in correct order.
- A body diagram or icon for each step.
- The "what breaks wudu" note box.
- Title: "How to Perform Wudu."
