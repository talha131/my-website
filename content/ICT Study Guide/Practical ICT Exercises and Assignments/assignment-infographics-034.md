Strategies for managing anxiety and worries.

## Objective

Create an infographic about managing anxiety and worries.

## Instructions

- State the purpose clearly: to give people practical strategies for coping with anxiety.
- Gather information from reputable sources such as psychology journals and mental health organizations.
- Keep it focused on steps that are easy to understand and apply.

## Background

- Use calming colors and imagery, such as nature scenes or soft gradients.

## Key Elements

- **Title:** Clear and direct, such as "Calm Your Mind: Strategies for Managing Anxiety."
- **Sections:** Organize by strategy type: mindfulness, relaxation exercises, and cognitive-behavioral methods.
- **Tips:** For each strategy, include specific actions and a short explanation of how they help.
- **Statistics:** Add relevant facts about how common anxiety is and how well certain coping methods work.
- **Quotes:** Include a short affirmation or motivating quote if it fits the design.

## Presentation

- Design a clean layout that guides the reader through the information in a clear order.
- Use readable fonts for both headings and body text.
- Add simple icons to represent each strategy visually.
- Leave enough white space to prevent the layout from feeling crowded.
