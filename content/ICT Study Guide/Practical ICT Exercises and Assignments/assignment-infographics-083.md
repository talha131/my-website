## Objective

Create a concept map in Canva that organizes different study skills and shows how they connect.

## Instructions

### Central Concept

Place **"Study Skills"** at the center.

### Main Branches

Connect at least five categories:

- Time Management
- Note-Taking
- Memory Techniques
- Reading Strategies
- Exam Preparation
- Focus and Concentration

### For Each Category, Add Specific Skills or Examples

- **Note-Taking:** Cornell method, mind mapping, outlining
- **Memory Techniques:** Spaced repetition, mnemonics, active recall
- **Time Management:** Study schedule, Pomodoro technique, prioritizing tasks
- **Exam Preparation:** Practicing past papers, revising summaries, timed practice

### Design in Canva

- Central node in the middle, categories on the first ring, specific skills on the outer ring.
- Distinct color for each category.
- Where two skills from different categories relate to each other, connect them with a dotted line.
- Keep each node to a short phrase.

### Required Elements

- At least 5 skill categories.
- At least 3 specific skills per category.
- At least one connecting line between skills from different branches.
- Title: "Study Skills."
