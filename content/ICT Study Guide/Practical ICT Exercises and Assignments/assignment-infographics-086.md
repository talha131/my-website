## Objective

Create a concept map in Canva that organizes the key elements of the Pakistan Movement - the political struggle that resulted in the creation of Pakistan on 14 August 1947.

## Instructions

### Central Concept

Place **"The Pakistan Movement"** at the center.

### Main Branches

Connect at least five categories:

- **Key Leaders:** Quaid-e-Azam Muhammad Ali Jinnah, Allama Iqbal, Liaquat Ali Khan, Fatima Jinnah
- **Key Events:** Lucknow Pact (1916), Khilafat Movement (1919-1924), Allahabad Address (1930), Lahore Resolution (1940), Cabinet Mission Plan (1946)
- **Political Organizations:** All-India Muslim League, Indian National Congress
- **Core Demand:** Two-Nation Theory, separate homeland for Muslims of the subcontinent
- **Obstacles:** Hindu-Muslim political tensions, British policies, Congress opposition to partition
- **Outcome:** Partition of 1947, independence of Pakistan and India on 14 and 15 August

### Design in Canva

- Central node with branches spreading outward.
- Color-code each category.
- Include years next to each key event.
- Use small portrait icons or symbols for key leaders.

### Required Elements

- At least 5 branches with 2 or more nodes each.
- Dates on all key events.
- Title: "The Pakistan Movement."
