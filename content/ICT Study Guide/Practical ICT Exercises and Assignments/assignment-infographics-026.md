How to make a perfect sandwich.

## Objective

Create an infographic about making the perfect sandwich.

## Instructions

- Choose a background that fits the food theme, such as a kitchen counter or cutting board.
- List the main components of a sandwich: bread, protein, vegetables, cheese, and condiments. Use icons or illustrations to represent each ingredient.
- Break the assembly process into clear, numbered steps. Add visuals for each step, such as spreading condiments or layering ingredients.
- Include practical tips, such as toasting the bread for extra crunch or layering ingredients in a specific order for better flavor.
- Suggest variations for different dietary preferences or flavor combinations.
- Finish with an image of the completed sandwich.
- Keep the infographic clear and simple, with a focus on easy-to-follow instructions.
