## Objective

Create a timeline infographic in Canva showing every FIFA World Cup winner from 1930 to 2022.

## Important Note on Accuracy

Look up the FIFA World Cup results online before adding any data. Verify the winning country, runner-up, host country, and final score for each tournament. Note that no tournament was held in 1942 or 1946 due to World War II.

## Instructions

### Step 1: Research

For each World Cup year - 1930, 1934, 1938, 1950, 1954, 1958, 1962, 1966, 1970, 1974, 1978, 1982, 1986, 1990, 1994, 1998, 2002, 2006, 2010, 2014, 2018, 2022 - find:

- Host country
- Winner
- Runner-up
- Score in the final

### Step 2: Design the Timeline

For each tournament:

- Year and host country (with flag)
- Winner (with flag)
- Runner-up
- Final score

Add a label between 1938 and 1950 noting the gap: "No tournament held 1942 and 1946 - World War II."

At the end of the timeline, include a count: which country has won the most titles, and how many?

### Step 3: Build in Canva

- Horizontal or vertical timeline from 1930 to 2022.
- Use flag icons for host and winning nations.
- Use football icons: a ball, a trophy.
- Apply subtle color bands to group decades.

### Required Elements

- All 22 tournaments included.
- Winner, runner-up, host country, and final score for each.
- WWII gap clearly noted.
- Total title count per country at the end.
- Title: "FIFA World Cup Winners: 1930-2022."
