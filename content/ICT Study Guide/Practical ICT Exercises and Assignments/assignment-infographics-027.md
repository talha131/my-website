Self-care checklist.

## Objective

Create an infographic about a self-care checklist.

## Background

- Use calming colors like pastel blues, greens, or soft neutrals to create a sense of relaxation.
- Keep the language clear and concise.
- Use icons or illustrations to represent each self-care activity.
- Organize the checklist by type: physical, emotional, and mental self-care.
- Use a clean, readable font at an appropriate size.

## Instructions

- Include a title that states the purpose clearly, such as "Self-Care Checklist."
- Write a short introduction explaining what self-care is and how the checklist helps people look after themselves.
- List each self-care activity with a short description of how to do it. For example:
    - "Practice deep breathing: Take slow, deep breaths for 5 minutes to reduce stress."
    - "Go for a walk: Spend 30 minutes outdoors to clear your mind."
- Add tips for fitting these activities into a daily routine.
- End with a note encouraging viewers to use the checklist regularly and adjust it to their needs.
