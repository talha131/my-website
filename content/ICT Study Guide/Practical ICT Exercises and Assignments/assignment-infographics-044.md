How to use traffic lights on the road.

## Objective

- Show the meaning of traffic light signals and how they apply to both pedestrians and vehicles.

## Instructions

- Cover the meaning of each light: red (stop), yellow (prepare to stop), and green (go).
- Add information about pedestrian traffic signals.

### Plan Your Infographic

- Section 1: Introduction to traffic lights.
- Section 2: Rules for drivers - what each color means.
- Section 3: Rules for pedestrians.

### Design Your Infographic

- Use the actual traffic light colors: red, yellow, and green.
- Include a simple diagram of traffic lights with labels.
