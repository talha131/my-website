## Objective

Create an infographic in Canva about the International Space Station (ISS), covering its structure, purpose, and role in space research.

## Instructions

Research the history, construction, and purpose of the ISS. Learn about its different modules and what each one is used for.

### Plan Your Infographic

- Outline key facts: size, orbit altitude, crew capacity.
- Plan sections for the ISS structure, daily life on board, and scientific experiments.

### Design in Canva

- Include a labeled diagram of the ISS structure.
- Use icons to represent different aspects of life and work on the station.
- Use a color scheme based on space and technology: blues, whites, and silvers.
- Choose a modern, tech-inspired font for headings and a clear font for details.
- Use a timeline to show the ISS construction and major milestones.

### Content to Include

- Information on international cooperation in the ISS project.
- How the ISS orbits Earth and how it is resupplied.
- Facts about daily life in space: how astronauts eat, sleep, and exercise.
- Key scientific experiments conducted on the ISS.

### Final Review

- Verify all facts and figures for accuracy.
- Make sure the infographic gives a clear overview of the ISS.
