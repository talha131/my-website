Climate change: cause, impact, and solutions

## Objective

Create an infographic that outlines the causes, impacts, and solutions to climate change.

## Instructions

- Include information about the current state of the climate crisis, with key facts and statistics about its causes, impacts, and the urgency for action.
- Visual Hierarchy: Use a clear visual hierarchy to guide the viewer through the information from most to least important, using size, color, and placement.
- Data Visualization: Present data through charts, graphs, and maps to make complex information easier to understand. Choose a visualization type that fits the data and the story you want to tell.
- Icons and Symbols: Use icons and symbols to represent key concepts. Choose symbols that are widely understood and relevant to the topic.
- Call to Action: Include a call to action to encourage viewers to take steps toward addressing climate change, such as reducing their carbon footprint or supporting renewable energy.
- Tool Selection: Use [Canva](https://www.canva.com/) to create your infographic.
- Layout: Plan the layout carefully, including the placement of text, images, and visual elements.
- Storytelling: Use the infographic to tell a clear story about the causes, impacts, and solutions to climate change. Frame the information in a way that connects with your audience.
- Engagement: Consider including interactive elements such as clickable links to support further engagement with the infographic.
