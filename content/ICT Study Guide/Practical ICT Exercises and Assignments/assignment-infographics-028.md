The science of sleep.

## Objective

Create an infographic about the science of sleep.

## Background

- Use a background that relates to sleep, such as a night sky or a quiet bedroom scene.
- Use calming colors like blue or purple.

## Key Elements

- **Sleep Stages:** Cover the different stages of sleep, including REM and non-REM sleep, and why each matters for rest.
- **Circadian Rhythm:** Explain the body's natural sleep-wake cycle and how it regulates sleep patterns.
- **Brain Activity:** Show how the brain behaves during sleep, including the role of dreams and memory.
- **Health Effects:** Show how sleep affects physical health, mental health, and immune function.
- **Sleep Disorders:** Briefly cover common issues like insomnia, sleep apnea, and narcolepsy.
- **Tips for Better Sleep:** List practical steps such as keeping a consistent sleep schedule and avoiding screens before bed.

## Presentation

- Use plain language to explain technical ideas.
- Add icons, illustrations, and diagrams for each key element.
- Arrange the information in a logical order so the viewer can follow it easily.
- Use charts or graphs where they help show data clearly.
- Balance text and visuals so neither dominates.

## Instructions

- Include specific advice, such as "Aim for 7-9 hours of sleep per night."
- Cite any sources used so readers can explore further.
