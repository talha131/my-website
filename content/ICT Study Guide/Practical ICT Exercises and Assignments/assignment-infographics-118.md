## Objective

Create a tips infographic in Canva showing practical ways to conserve water at home, designed for families in Karachi and other Pakistani cities where water is often scarce.

## Instructions

### Tips to Include

Present each tip with a short heading, a one or two sentence explanation, and an icon:

1. **Fix leaking taps immediately** - A dripping tap can waste over 20 liters of water a day. Report leaks to your building management or fix them yourself.
2. **Use a bucket, not a running tap, for washing vehicles** - Running a hose wastes far more water than needed to wash a car or motorbike.
3. **Take shorter showers** - Reducing a shower from 10 minutes to 5 can save significant water daily.
4. **Turn off the tap while brushing teeth** - Running water during brushing wastes several liters each time.
5. **Only run the washing machine with a full load** - Half-empty loads use nearly the same amount of water as full ones.
6. **Reuse washing water for plants** - Rinse water from vegetables or light laundry can water plants instead of going down the drain.
7. **Store water properly** - Keep water tanks clean and covered to prevent contamination and evaporation.
8. **Report water waste in your area** - If a street pipe is leaking or a neighbor is wasting water, report it to KWSB (Karachi Water and Sewerage Board) or the building manager.

### Add a Context Note

Include a short note: Karachi receives water from the Hub Dam, Keenjhar Lake, and the Indus River. The city's water demand consistently exceeds its supply. Every liter saved matters.

### Design in Canva

- Grid or list layout with one panel per tip.
- Bright blue and white color scheme.
- Each tip has a bold heading and a relevant icon (a tap, a bucket, a washing machine).
- The context note in a separate highlighted box.

### Required Elements

- At least 7 tips with icons.
- A local context note about Karachi's water situation.
- Title: "Save Water at Home."
