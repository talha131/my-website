## Objective

Create a concept map in Canva that maps the components of a healthy lifestyle and shows how they connect.

## Instructions

### Central Concept

Place **"Healthy Lifestyle"** at the center.

### Main Branches

Connect at least five pillars:

- Nutrition and Diet
- Physical Activity
- Sleep and Rest
- Mental and Emotional Health
- Personal Hygiene
- Social Connections

### For Each Pillar, Add Specific Habits

- **Nutrition:** Eating vegetables daily, limiting sugary drinks, drinking enough water
- **Physical Activity:** Walking 30 minutes daily, stretching, playing a sport
- **Sleep:** 7-8 hours per night, a consistent sleep time, avoiding screens before bed
- **Mental Health:** Daily prayer and reflection, talking to someone you trust, reducing unnecessary stress

Include habits that are practical for university students in Karachi.

### Design in Canva

- One central node with six branches spreading outward.
- Each pillar gets its own color.
- Add an icon next to each pillar label (a plate for nutrition, a running figure for exercise).
- Where two pillars influence each other (e.g., sleep and mental health), connect them with a dotted line.

### Required Elements

- At least 5 lifestyle pillars.
- At least 3 specific habits per pillar.
- Icons on each pillar node.
- Title: "Healthy Lifestyle."
