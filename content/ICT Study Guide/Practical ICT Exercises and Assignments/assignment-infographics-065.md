## Objective

Create an infographic in Canva about meteor showers - their causes, types, and how to observe them.

## Instructions

Research the difference between meteors, meteoroids, and meteorites. Understand how meteor showers connect to comet debris trails.

### Plan Your Infographic

- Outline the process from meteoroid to meteorite.
- Plan sections for famous meteor showers and their parent comets.

### Design in Canva

- Create a diagram showing Earth passing through a comet's debris field.
- Use icons to represent the different types of meteorites: iron, stone, and stony-iron.
- Use a dark background with bright streaks to suggest a meteor shower.
- Choose a dynamic, space-themed font for headings and a clear font for details.
- Create a timeline of major annual meteor showers.
- Use a circular diagram showing Earth's orbit and the locations of major showers.

### Content to Include

- How to observe meteor showers safely.
- Information on the Perseid and Leonid showers.
- Interesting facts about meteorites found on Earth.
- The difference between meteors and fireballs.

### Final Review

- Verify all astronomical data and shower dates.
- Make sure the infographic is clear and accurate.
