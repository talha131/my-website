The life cycle of a butterfly.

## Objective

Create an infographic on the life cycle of a butterfly.

## Instructions

- Research the four stages of a butterfly's life cycle before you begin designing.
- Decide on a layout that moves logically from one stage to the next.
- Collect relevant images, icons, or illustrations.
- Use Canva to build the infographic.
- Write short text descriptions for each stage.

## Background

- Briefly introduce the butterfly life cycle and its place in biology and ecology.

## Key Elements

- **Egg Stage:** An illustration of butterfly eggs with a brief description.
- **Larva (Caterpillar) Stage:** An image of a caterpillar with notes on its feeding habits and growth.
- **Pupa (Chrysalis) Stage:** An illustration of a chrysalis with an explanation of metamorphosis.
- **Adult Butterfly Stage:** An image of a butterfly with a description of its behavior and reproduction.
- **Extra:** A fun fact, such as information on species diversity or migration.

## Presentation

- Use bright colors and clear visuals.
- Keep the layout easy to follow from one stage to the next.
- Use short text with bullet points or brief paragraphs.
- Include a clear title such as "The Life Cycle of a Butterfly."
