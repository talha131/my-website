## Objective

Create an infographic in Canva that covers the plot, important events, and main characters of "The Incredibles" (2004).

## Instructions

Watch "The Incredibles" (2004) if you haven't seen it recently. Take notes on the main plot points, key events, and important characters.

### Plan Your Infographic

- Outline the main characters: Mr. Incredible, Elastigirl, Violet, Dash, Jack-Jack, and Syndrome.
- Identify 5-7 major plot points in the Parr family's superhero journey.
- Note key locations like their home, Syndrome's island, and Metroville.

### Design in Canva

- Use superhero-themed icons and symbols.
- Use bold colors from the Incredibles' costumes: red, black, and yellow.
- Choose a dynamic, comic-book style font for headings and a clear font for details.
- Create a comic book panel layout for different scenes, and use a family tree style to introduce characters and their superpowers.

### Content to Include

- Movie title "The Incredibles" and release year (2004).
- Brief descriptions of the main characters and their superpowers.
- A memorable quote such as "No capes!"
- The central themes of family teamwork and embracing one's identity.

### Final Review

- Check for spelling and grammar mistakes.
- Confirm that all information accurately reflects the movie's plot.
- Make sure the infographic is easy to read.
