## Objective

Create an infographic in Canva that covers the plot, important events, and main characters of "Zootopia" (2016).

## Instructions

Watch "Zootopia" (2016) if you haven't seen it recently. Take notes on the main plot points, key events, and important characters.

### Plan Your Infographic

- Outline the main characters: Judy Hopps, Nick Wilde, and other key animals.
- Identify 5-7 major plot points in Judy's journey as a police officer.
- Note the various districts of Zootopia (Tundratown, Sahara Square, etc.).

### Design in Canva

- Use animal icons to represent different characters.
- Use a diverse color palette that reflects the different districts.
- Choose a modern, sleek font for headings and a clear font for details.
- Create a city map layout showing the different districts and where events occur.

### Content to Include

- Movie title "Zootopia" and release year (2016).
- Brief descriptions of the main characters and their roles in the city.
- A memorable quote such as "Try everything!"
- The central themes of overcoming prejudice and following your goals.

### Final Review

- Check for spelling and grammar mistakes.
- Confirm that all information accurately reflects the movie's plot.
- Make sure the infographic is easy to read.
