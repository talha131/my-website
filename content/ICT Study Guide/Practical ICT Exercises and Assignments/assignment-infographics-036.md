How to maintain a clean and healthy space.

## Objective

Create an infographic about maintaining a clean and healthy space.

## Instructions

- Decide on the main points: cleanliness, organization, disinfection, and mental well-being.
- Find relevant tips and statistics from credible health sources.
- Use Canva to design the infographic.
- Choose a layout, color scheme, and typography that fit the topic.

## Background

- Explain why keeping a clean and tidy space matters for preventing the spread of germs and maintaining overall well-being.

## Key Elements

- **Cleaning Tips:** Practical advice for cleaning kitchens, bathrooms, and living areas.
- **Organization Tips:** Suggestions for decluttering and organizing spaces to reduce stress.
- **Disinfection Guidelines:** Correct techniques for disinfecting surfaces like doorknobs, light switches, and electronics.
- **Mental Health Tips:** Steps for staying mentally well at home, such as building a daily routine and staying connected with others.

## Presentation

- Use a clear layout with distinct sections.
- Add icons, illustrations, or photographs to support each point.
- Keep text short and easy to read, using bullet points or numbered lists.
- Include the sources you used to give the information credibility.
