The Chinese Whisper Game

## Objective

Create an infographic in Canva that explains the Chinese Whisper game (also known as Telephone) - its rules, how it works, and why it is useful for building listening skills and teamwork.

## Instructions

Learn about:

- What the Chinese Whisper game is and how it is played.
- The key rules and what makes it enjoyable.
- The purpose of the game: team-building and improving listening skills.

### Plan Your Infographic

Organize it into sections:

- What is the Chinese Whisper Game? (brief introduction)
- How to Play (step-by-step instructions)
- Why Play It? (benefits for teamwork and listening)

### Design in Canva

- Use speech bubbles to show how a sentence is whispered from person to person.
- Include figures or cartoons passing a message along a line.
- Use bright, cheerful colors such as yellow, pink, and green.
- Choose a fun, readable font.
- Arrange the steps with illustrations or diagrams so the process is easy to follow.

### Content to Include

- Tips for making the game more fun, such as funny phrases to whisper.
- The benefits of the game: active listening and teamwork.

### Final Review

- Check for spelling mistakes.
- Make sure the message flow reads clearly from start to finish.
