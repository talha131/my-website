## Objective

Create an infographic in Canva that covers the major robotic missions to Mars, from the 1960s to the present.

## Instructions

Research major Mars missions from the 1960s to today, covering both successful missions and notable failures.

### Plan Your Infographic

Outline 8-10 significant Mars missions - for example: Mariner 4, Viking 1 and 2, Mars Pathfinder, Curiosity rover. For each mission, note the launch date, arrival date, and main objectives.

### Design in Canva

- Use Mars-inspired colors and textures: reds, oranges, and browns.
- Include images or illustrations of the spacecraft and rovers.
- Choose a clear, modern font for easy reading.
- Use a combination of timeline and grid layout to show how missions progressed over time.

### Content to Include

- Key discoveries from each mission.
- Interesting facts about Mars (size compared to Earth, length of a Martian year).
- A section on future planned Mars missions, if applicable.

### Final Review

- Verify all mission data and discoveries for accuracy.
- Make sure the infographic tells a clear story of Mars exploration from beginning to present.
